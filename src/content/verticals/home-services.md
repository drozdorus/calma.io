## High-ticket projects, high-intent homeowners

Home improvement is a high-consideration, high-ticket category: a new roof, a solar install,
or a full window replacement is a major decision a homeowner researches before committing.
That makes a genuinely in-market homeowner — and the timing of when they enter the market —
the unit that contractors and installers compete to reach first.

## How Calma generates home services leads

We run user-acquisition campaigns across paid social and video, then qualify and route the
resulting homeowners to buyers. AI identifies the audiences most likely to be planning a
project; creative is tested continuously and optimized to cost per **qualified** lead; and
every record is checked for authenticity, consent, and project fit before it leaves our
system. The result is a lead stream measured by contact rate and close rate — not raw volume.

## Quality and consent built in

Even outside the most heavily regulated verticals, lead quality and consent decide whether a
campaign actually pays back. We build consent capture and verification into the funnel,
screen for homeownership and project relevance, and design creative and measurement to be
compliance-first — so the leads we deliver are real, reachable, and worth quoting.
